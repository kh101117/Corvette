Corvette C8 Series

All the Power. All the Style. All in One Place.

C8 Stingray

Engine: 6.2L V8

Horsepower: 490 hp (495 hp w/ performance exhaust)

0–60 mph: 2.9 seconds

Top Speed: 194 mph

Drivetrain: RWD

Price: $63,000

The C8 Stingray is the base model of the eighth-generation Corvette, offering a mid-engine layout, a 6.2L V8 engine, and stunning performance at an incredible value.

C8 Z51

Engine: 6.2L V8

Horsepower: 495 hp

0–60 mph: 2.8 seconds

Top Speed: ~184 mph

Upgrades: Performance exhaust, suspension, brakes, ELSD

Drivetrain: RWD

Price: $71,000

The Z51 Performance Package adds upgraded suspension, brakes, an electronic limited-slip differential, performance exhaust, and more track-focused features to the base Stingray.

C8 Z06

Engine: 5.5L flat-plane V8

Horsepower: 670 hp

0–60 mph: 2.6 seconds

Top Speed: 195+ mph

Drivetrain: RWD

Price: $106,000

The C8 Z06 boasts a naturally aspirated 5.5L flat-plane crank V8 engine that delivers over 670 horsepower, making it the most track-capable Corvette ever made.

C8 E-Ray

Engine: 6.2L V8 + Electric Motor

Total Power: 655 hp

0–60 mph: 2.5 seconds

Top Speed: 183 mph

Drivetrain: AWD

Price: $104,000

The C8 E-Ray is the first hybrid Corvette, combining the classic V8 with electric motors to provide all-wheel drive and quicker acceleration than any previous model.

C8 ZR1

Engine: Twin-turbo 5.5L V8

Estimated Horsepower: 850–900 hp

0–60 mph: ~2.5 seconds (est.)

Top Speed: ~210+ mph (est.)

Drivetrain: RWD

Price: $150,000

The C8 ZR1 is the ultimate performance C8, it features a twin-turbocharged version of the Z06's engine and pushes Corvette engineering to new heights.
